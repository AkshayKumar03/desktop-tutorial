SIX CRICKET

SIX Cricket Academy runs the cricket programme at AKS with a strong focus on the spirit of ‘Cricket for All. The Academy aims to provide India’s cricket loving public with access to top-class cricket facilities, knowledge, programs and events, and create an atmosphere that facilitates learning through fun and enjoyment.
