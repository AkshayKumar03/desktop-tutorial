EXPERIENCE THE CLUB@AKS.

Sometimes the only way to move forward is to take a step back. Refresh the mind. Rejuvenate the body. Re-fuel for the race ahead. So step back in style, with an exclusive membership at The Club@AKS.

EXPERIENCE STATE-OF-THE-ART SPORT.

Located within the prestigious Akshay Kumar Centre for Sports Excellence (AKS), The Club@AKS is home to world-class facilities in Badminton, Cricket, Football, Tennis, Swimming, Squash and Basketball. Select a sport and play in an arena designed for the finest athletes in the country.

EXPERIENCE PRIVILEGE.

The Club@AKS is your refuge from the world. Enjoy privileged access to a lively schedule of social and sporting events at AKS. Mingle with friends and family, develop social and business connections or just unwind in the elegant environs of our Members’ Lounge nestled atop the Clubhouse, with a panoramic view of AKS’s verdant premises. End your day at our fine dining restaurant, sipping on a drink and savouring a delicious meal crafted to tickle the palate of the most discerning gourmand.

EXPERIENCE TEAM SPIRIT.

Reward your team with the benefits of an active and healthy lifestyle, with a Corporate Membership. Motivate them to take up an individual sport or participate in tailor-made group classes, team-building exercises and fitness challenges. Host corporate sporting events, conferences and workshops at our well-appointed meeting rooms, fully equipped Media Centre and large event venues, offered at specially discounted rates for members.
