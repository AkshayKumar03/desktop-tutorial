THE COMPLETE SPORTING EXPERIENCE…AND MORE!

AKS’s sprawling 15-acre sports campus is the perfect venue for any corporate event - from sports day, family day or the company picnic to off-site team-building exercises, inter-departmental tournaments, workshops and conferences. Fun in the sun with team sports, fast-paced indoor games to exercise mind and body or a wholistic approach to health and wellness, AKS has something for everyone. In addition to customised sporting activities organised for small and large groups, AKS also offers well-appointed meeting areas, dining options and retail outlets to our clients. Meeting Halls at AKS are ideal for formal corporate meetings and conferences, and our cutting-edge Media Centre complete with the best audiovisual equipment can be used for presentations, workshops and briefings. Our outdoor Amphitheatre is the perfect setting for staged performances, artistic or cultural events.
