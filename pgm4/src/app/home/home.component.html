SPORTS SCIENCE & FITNESS PARTNERSS

PSTP

The Padhma Shree Targeting Performance Centre utilises cutting edge technology to enhance performance training, conditioning and injury rehabilitation in athletes.

VESOMA

The Vesoma Fitness, Rehabilitation and Sports Medical Centre caters to the needs of elite and recreational sportspersons with a top-notch Gym & Fitness Centre as well as Physiotherapy, Nutrition and allied services.

PS FIT

The PS Fit Centre at AKS offers alternative fitness formats such as boxing, zumba and yoga, to suit the needs of every exercise enthusiast.

LITTLE GYM

Also on the premises is The Little Gym, which offers engaging fitness activities for children including gymnastics. karate, kids’ dance, parent/child classes and other sports skills.
